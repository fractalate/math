% Cracking the GRE Mathematics Subject Test 4e, Chapter 1, Question 13
\problem When the polynomial $p(x)$ is divided by $x-1$, it leaves a remainder of 1, and when $p(x)$ is divided by $x+1$, it leaves a remainder of -1. Find the remainder when $p(x)$ is divided by $x^2-1$?
\\

\begin{tabular}{ccccccccccc}
  (A) & $-1$
  &(B) & $0$
  &(C) & $x$
  &(D) & $-x$
  &(E) & $2x$
\end{tabular}

\problemsep

% Cracking the GRE Mathematics Subject Test 4e, Chapter 1, Question 15
\problem If the roots of the equation $x^2 + Bx + 1 = 0$ are the squares of the roots of the equation $x^2 + bx + 1 = 0$, which of the following expresses $B$ in terms of $b$?
\\

\begin{tabular}{ccccccccccc}
  (A) & $2 - b^2$
  &(B) & $1 - b^2$
  &(C) & $b^2 - 1$
  &(D) & $b^2$
  &(E) & $b^2 - 2$
\end{tabular}

\problemsep
